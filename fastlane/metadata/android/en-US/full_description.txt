Openshelf is a free, open-source personal library manager. It functions as a comprehensive reading tracker, organizer (by category, collection, imprint, or any combination), and book archival tool. Everything stays on your device—no account required, and no data ever leaves your phone.

Features:
- Dynamic Views: Instant toggle between List and Grid layouts.
- Reading Status: Track Want to read, Reading, Read, Abandoned, and Paused states.
- Powerful Search: Full-text search across Title, Author, ISBN, and personal notes.
- Custom Covers: Search via Open Library, Inventaire, or use custom photos.
- Smart Shelves: Save filter combinations as self-updating shelves.
- Hybrid FOSS Scanner: High-speed barcode detection and ISBN OCR recognition.
- Detailed Statistics: Reorganizable widgets for tracking reading streaks, page goals, and reading history.

Openshelf is designed for book lovers who care about the craftsmanship of managing a personal collection. 100% on-device and privacy-focused.
